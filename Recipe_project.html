Microwave Cake

There is perhaps nothing better than a warm comfy microwave chocolate cake.Here is one of my favourite recipes!!

List of Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantity
Softened Butter 3 tablespoons
White Sugar 1/4 cup
Egg 1
Vanilla extract 1 tablespoon
Milk 1/4 Cup
All-purpose flour ½ cup
Cocoa powder 2 tablespoons
baking powder ¼ teaspoon
Salt 1 pinch

Steps:

  1. Spray a medium microwave-safe bowl with non-stick spray and set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, and milk; blend together.
  3. Combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt; blend into the mixture until smooth. Pour into greased bowl.
  4. Cover and cook in the microwave on high for 2 to 2 1/2 minutes, or until cake springs back when touched.
  5. adjust the cooking time to accommodate your machine.
  6. Serve and enjoy!!

Pizza

Pizza, the king of comfort foods. Try this simple, taste sensation.

List of Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantity
Pizza sauce 3 tbsp
Thinly sliced mushrooms 5
Roughly chopped onion () 3 tbsp
Roughly chopped capsicum ¼ cup
Jalapeños 9 pickled
Chopped black olives ¼ cup
Grated mozzarella cheese ½ cup
oregano / italian seasonings / mixed herbs ½ tsp
Pizza dough -

Steps:

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. place the dough on dusted pizza plate or tray.
  3. flatten the dough by stretching with both hands.
  4. further, leaving a cm or more and create a dent.
  5. then prick with centre of dough with the help of fork.
  6. furthermore, spread pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly.
  7. also top with topping of choice.
  8. then spread grated cheese and sprinkle italian seasonings.
  9. further, bake at 500 degrees fahrenheit for about 12 - 15 minutes.
  10. finally, slice and serve pizza hot.

Salad

Who can resist a fresh salad ! Here's one of my favorites.

List of Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantity
Shredded red and/or green cabbage 6 to 7 cups
Mix of peppers: You can use 3 Anaheim and 3 banana peppers; or use 1 red bell and 1 poblano. -
Chopped scallions, 2
Chopped cilantro, including stems 1/2 cup
Fresh basil, Thai basil, and/or mint ½ cup
Thai chiles or serrano pepper, diced 2 and 1 respectively
Toasted peanuts, pepitas, and/or sesame seeds ¼ cup
Ripe peach, thinly sliced 1
Dressing -
Sea Salt 1 pinch

Steps:

  1. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the cashew butter, miso paste, lime juice, sesame oil, and ginger. Whisk in enough water to create a drizzable consistency. Set aside.
  2. In a dry cast-iron skillet over medium heat, char the peppers whole, rotating until the edges have a little char, about 2 minutes per side. Remove. When cool to the touch, slice in half lengthwise, remove the stem, ribbing, and seeds and slice horizontally into thin strips
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, peppers, scallions, cilantro, basil, chiles, and ¾ of the dressing. Toss until combined. Add the remaining dressing, if desired, and season to taste with a few pinches of sea salt. Serve topped with the toasted nuts, seeds, and sliced peaches.
